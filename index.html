There lived a boy who wanted to be a sailor

The boy came from the suburb of the Sundarbans. All his childhood he wanted to be a sailor. Instead, he got stuck in a lighthouse where he observes the sunsets.

This is called life, perhaps, he thinks. Or, maybe someday he will really fly over the seven seas. Freely. With joyous excitement.

Did he want to be a sailor indeed? Or, he just wanted to be a poet? A bard, perhaps?

Being a poet is not less analogous to being a sailor, he thinks. And yet he sits on the shore to see the sun sets over the seas.
